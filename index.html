    ABOUT US

    At Bon Appetitt, we believe that dining is more than just a meal – it's an experience to be savored, shared, and remembered. Our story began with a passion for culinary excellence and a desire to create a haven where food lovers could indulge in the finest flavors in an atmosphere of warmth and hospitality. We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we work closely with local farmers and artisans to source the freshest, highest quality produce, meats, and seafood. Our dedication to quality is evident in every dish we serve.

    Our chefs are masters of their craft, blending traditional techniques with innovative flair to create dishes that are both delicious and unique. From classic favorites to inspired culinary creations, there's something on our menu to delight every palate. In addition to our main dining area, we also offer a private dining room for special events and gatherings. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or corporate event, our team is here to help you create an unforgettable experience tailored to your needs.

    Bagels with Lox and Cream Cheese

    NYC is renowned for its bagels, especially when paired with smoked salmon (lox) and cream cheese.

    Handcrafted with care and precision, our bagels are freshly baked to perfection, boasting that quintessential chewy texture and subtle crust. Each bagel is generously slathered with our rich and creamy house-made cream cheese, creating a luxurious base for the star of the show: premium hand-sliced Nova Scotia lox.
